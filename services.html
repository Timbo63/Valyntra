How We Work

We do not start with tools.

We start with your workflows, decisions, and operational constraints. Only then do we identify where AI creates defensible, measurable value.

Every engagement is scoped clearly, delivered personally by Timothy Sharp, and structured to give you something actionable — not a report that sits on a shelf.

Start with the Diagnostic. Before any advisory or implementation work, we need to understand your operations. The Diagnostic is a short, structured engagement that produces a written report — your AI readiness, top opportunities, and where not to invest.

Move at the pace that fits your business. Some clients take the Diagnostic and execute independently. Others move into Advisory or Implementation. There is no pressure to do more than you need.

No outsourcing. No generic outputs. Every evaluation, recommendation, and engagement is handled directly by Timothy Sharp. What you receive reflects your business — not a template.

What We Offer

Four ways to engage.

Each service is designed for a specific stage of your AI journey. Most clients begin with the Diagnostic — it informs everything that follows.

01
Entry Point

AI Diagnostic

A structured, short-form engagement that produces a written AI Opportunity Report. Designed to give leadership a clear, expert view of where AI fits — and where it does not — before any investment is made.

What it is
A defined, fixed-scope engagement. You complete an intake form, we analyze your workflows and operational context, and deliver a written report within 5 business days.
What you get
AI Readiness Score · Problem-to-opportunity mapping · Top 3–5 ranked AI opportunities · ROI snapshot · Build vs buy vs automate guidance · What not to do · 30-day action plan
When to use
Before investing in AI tools, hiring AI talent, or committing budget to any AI initiative. Also useful if previous AI efforts have underdelivered.
You walk away with
A written PDF report. Complete and actionable on its own. Transparent flat fee. No retainer required.

AI Advisory

A recurring engagement — monthly or quarterly — focused on AI prioritization, workflow alignment, and decision support as your operations evolve.
What you get
Ongoing AI opportunity review · Workflow and pipeline alignment · Vendor-neutral guidance · Decision support for leadership · Progress tracking against your 30-day action plan

Execution

AI Implementation

Turning strategy into working systems. For organizations that have completed the Diagnostic and are ready to move from recommendations to deployment.

Optional Path

Partner Match

When Valyntra is not the right execution partner, we introduce vetted vendors suited to your specific needs — and maintain oversight of the engagement if requested.
