Real World Application

What diagnostics uncover in practice.

The following case study shows the kind of structural insight a diagnostic can surface — applied through PRISM, Valyntra's AI-enabled revenue intelligence framework.

The Situation

Medical device company. Healthy pipeline on paper. Revenue diverging from forecast.

Traditional CRM indicators showed minimal risk. Leadership had no clear explanation for forecast instability. The organization lacked a way to correlate sales execution, clinical engagement, and reimbursement signals.

What the Diagnostic Found

A large portion of the late-stage pipeline carried hidden risk not visible in CRM data.

Multi-signal risk scoring across sales, clinical, and reimbursement indicators revealed the growth forecast depended on deals whose stability was materially overstated by traditional reporting.

Early

Zero
System integration required — CSV-based, no PHI exposure

Shift from reactive reporting to predictive risk management
